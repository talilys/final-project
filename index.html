Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint.

It takes place in Seoul, South Korea. Omniscient Reader’s Viewpoint depicts in the form of scenarios where people had to survive by clearing each of the scenarios presented by the star stream.

When the world is faced with destruction, only one man knows how it ends—because he is the Omniscient Reader. Kim Dokja is the only reader who has finished the whole book *Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined World*, which was written and published over the course of 10 years by the anonymous author tls123. Kim Dokja's specialized understanding of the book is essential to his survival when the *Ways of Survival* principle is applied to the actual world.
